May's pancake recipe

How to make MAY PARKER'S FAMOUS PANCAKES

Ingredients

  • flour
  • yeast
  • buttermilk
  • sodium bicarbonate
  • eggs
  • butter
  • salt

preparation

  1. It mixes:
  2. 1 cup black wheat flour
  3. 1 cup whole wheat flour
  4. 2 tablespoons yeast powderv
  5. 1 tablespoon baking soda
  6. 1 tablespoon of salt
  7. In a separate bowl, mix 2 cups of buttermilk and 2 tablespoons of molasses.
  8. Add to the flour mixture:
  9. 2 beaten egg yolks
  10. 1/4 cup melted butter
  11. the mixture of buttermilk and molasses.
  12. Two egg whites are beaten until they set (just enough!), and then they are carefully mixed into the dough.
  13. Fry the dough on a griddle or pan until small bubbles appear on the surface. Then turn it over and leave it in the pan until the bottom browns slightly.
  14. The pancakes are served hot, with butter and maple syrup...Peter's favorite!
