May's pancake recipe

How to make MAY PARKER'S FAMOUS PANCAKES

  • It mixes:
  • 1 cup black wheat flour
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons yeast powderv
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • In a separate bowl, mix 2 cups of buttermilk and 2 tablespoons of molasses.
  • Add to the flour mixture:
  • 2 beaten egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • the mixture of buttermilk and molasses.
  • Two egg whites are beaten until they set (just enough!), and then they are carefully mixed into the dough.
  • Fry the dough on a griddle or pan until small bubbles appear on the surface. Then turn it over and leave it in the pan until the bottom browns slightly.
  • The pancakes are served hot, with butter and maple syrup...Peter's favorite!
