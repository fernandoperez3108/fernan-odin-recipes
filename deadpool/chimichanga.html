How to make a chimichanga?

Ingredients

  • Wheat tortillas
  • Mince
  • Onion
  • diced tomato
  • Zanahoria rallada
  • Spoonful jalapeno pepper, sliced
  • Cooked black beans
  • Mozzarella cheese or other melting cheese
  • Shall
  • Ground black pepper
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Sour cream
  • Finely chopped onion
  • Tomato in small cubes
  • Grated carrot
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Lime

How to make chimichanga

  1. Put the oil with the chopped onion and carrot in a large frying pan and fry over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. After that time we add the tomato and fry for five more minutes. Add the minced meat, increase the heat to medium-high and cook everything for 10 minutes, stirring so that the meat does not become caked.

  3. Add the beans and jalapenos and cook for five more minutes. Season with salt and pepper and set aside off the heat in a bowl.

  4. We arrange a wheat tortilla on a board, fill it with two heaping tablespoons of filling, put the cheese on top to melt and close it like a burrito, tightly. So with all chimichangas.

  5. In hot oil, at about 160-175 ºC, we fry the pieces that we have prepared. To secure the filling we can close them with some toothpicks so that they do not open when we put them in to fry.

  6. In hot oil, at about 160-175 ºC, we fry the pieces that we have prepared. To secure the filling we can close them with some toothpicks so that they do not open when we put them in to fry.

  7. We serve the chimichangas on a plate and put two tablespoons of sour cream and a little of the chopped raw vegetable mixture on top. We finish the plating with some cilantro leaves and grating the peel of a lime on top.
